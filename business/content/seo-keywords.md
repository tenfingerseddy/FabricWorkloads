> Keyword research and targeting strategy for the Fabric observability niche.
> Last updated: March 2026

---

## Keyword Research Methodology

Search volume estimates are based on a combination of Google Keyword Planner ranges, Ahrefs/SEMrush category benchmarks for enterprise SaaS and data engineering tools, and manual analysis of search results page competition. Microsoft Fabric is a growing but still niche platform, so many keywords have low absolute volume but extremely high intent and conversion potential. The audience is small but highly qualified: data engineers and platform teams with purchasing influence.

Volume indicators used below:
- **Very Low**: <100 monthly searches
- **Low**: 100-500 monthly searches
- **Medium**: 500-2,000 monthly searches
- **High**: 2,000-10,000 monthly searches
- **Very High**: >10,000 monthly searches

### Why These Keywords Matter

The "Microsoft Fabric monitoring" cluster is where we have the clearest path to page-one ranking. Competition is low because:
1. No dedicated observability vendor is producing Fabric-specific SEO content at scale
2. Microsoft's own documentation ranks but does not address the pain points we cover
3. Community blog posts on these topics are sparse and non-recurring

Our strategy is to own the "Fabric observability" search territory through consistent, high-quality content that addresses real operational pain points.

## On-Page SEO Checklist (Per Blog Post)

- [ ] Primary keyword in the H1 title
- [ ] Primary keyword in the first 100 words
- [ ] Primary keyword in at least one H2 heading
- [ ] Primary keyword density: 0.5-1.5% (natural usage, not forced)
- [ ] Secondary keywords distributed across H2/H3 headings
- [ ] Meta description contains primary keyword and is 150-160 characters
- [ ] URL slug is short and keyword-rich (dev.to auto-generates from title)
- [ ] At least one internal link to a previous blog post
- [ ] At least one external link to an authoritative source (Microsoft docs, GitHub)
- [ ] Image alt text includes the primary or secondary keyword where natural
- [ ] Post length exceeds 1,500 words (minimum for SEO ranking potential)
